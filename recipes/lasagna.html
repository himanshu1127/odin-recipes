Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Make the ricotta mixture
  3. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  4. Cover with foil and bake
  5. Let the lasagna rest before serving

How to Layer Lasagna

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top whit the remaining Parmesan
